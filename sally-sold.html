Sally Sold

Stunning invoice templates for freelancers. Pick a template, fill in the details, and download a PDF that looks like Apple designed it. Free to use — sign in to save your details and download PDFs.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

This privacy policy describes how 47 Summers LLC ("we", "us") handles information in connection with Sally Sold.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google or Facebook, we receive your name, email address, and profile image from those providers. We do not receive or store your password.
  • Invoice data: Sally Sold stores the invoices you create, including business details, client information, line items, and amounts. This data is stored on our servers so you can access it across devices.
  • Contact information: If you email us, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.
  • Crash data and diagnostics: We collect basic error logs to diagnose and fix bugs. These logs do not contain your invoice content.

We do not collect analytics, tracking identifiers, or location information.

How We Use Your Information

Account information is used to authenticate you and display your profile. Invoice data is used solely to provide the service — generating, storing, and retrieving your invoices. Contact information is used to respond to your inquiries.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Sally Sold does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Third-Party Services

  • Authentication: Google and Facebook OAuth are used for sign-in. Their privacy policies govern data they collect during the sign-in flow.
  • PDF generation: Invoices are rendered to PDF on our servers. Your invoice data is processed in memory during generation and is not retained by the rendering service.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing us. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.

Children's Privacy

Sally Sold is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date above.
