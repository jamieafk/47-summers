Swish

Quick-capture notes app for iOS. Type a note, swipe up, and it saves as a Markdown file to any folder you choose — iCloud, Dropbox, or anywhere your device can reach.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

This privacy policy describes how 47 Summers LLC ("we", "us") handles information in connection with Swish.

Information We Collect

Swish is designed to collect as little data as possible. Specifically:

  • Your notes: Swish stores your notes on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We do not have access to your notes.
  • Contact information: If you email us, we receive your email address and the contents of your message.
  • Crash data and diagnostics: If you have opted in through your device settings, Apple may share anonymous crash logs and diagnostics with us. This data does not identify you personally.

We do not collect analytics, usage data, tracking identifiers, or location information.

How We Use Your Information

Contact information is used solely to respond to your inquiries. Crash data, if shared, is used to fix bugs.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Swish does not include third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your notes live on your device and your iCloud account — we never have them. If you email us, we retain the correspondence only as long as needed to resolve your inquiry. You can request deletion of any data we hold by emailing us.

Children's Privacy

Swish is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date above.
